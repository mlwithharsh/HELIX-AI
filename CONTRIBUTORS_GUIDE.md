# Contributor Roles and Expectations

HELIX AI is categorized into specialized domains. While everyone is encouraged to explore the full stack, specialized roles help maintain high quality in core subsystems.

## AI/ML Engineer
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Optimizing local GGUF model inference performance.
    - Implementing and refining specialized AI personalities.
    - Developing the RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) pipeline.
    - Researching and integrating new LLM architectures.
- **Expectations**: Deep understanding of transformer architectures, quantization methods, and local inference engines like llama.cpp.

## Backend Developer
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Architecting and maintaining the FastAPI core.
    - Implementing secure and efficient REST and SSE endpoints.
    - Managing database schemas and migrations.
    - Improving the resilience of the Adaptive Orchestrator.
- **Expectations**: Proficiency in Python, asynchronous programming, and database management (PostgreSQL/Supabase).

## Voice Systems Engineer
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Maintaining the Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) integration.
    - Optimizing audio processing latency.
    - Implementing streaming audio pipelines.
- **Expectations**: Experience with audio processing libraries (e.g., Whisper, Coqui, ElevenLabs) and real-time data streaming.

## Frontend Developer
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Building premium, responsive React components.
    - Crafting high-quality animations and interactive states.
    - Optimizing frontend performance for low-power devices.
- **Expectations**: Strong skills in React, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion.

## Quality Assurance & Tester
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Designing automated testing suites for both frontend and backend.
    - Performing edge-case validation for local GGUF models.
    - Auditing the system for security vulnerabilities.
- **Expectations**: Detail-oriented mindset, experience with Pytest, Vitest, and Playwright/Cypress.

---
Regardless of your role, all contributors are expected to uphold the standards in `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
